TOC/TN pharma

Model: multi N/C 3100

Specification:

Models: multi N/C 3100 pharma - TOC/TN analyzer for pharmaceutical applications

Measuring Time: 3 - 5 min

Digestion Mode: High temperature combustion up to 950 oC

Precision: 1 - 2 %

Injection Volume: 100 - 1000 µL

Detector: Focus Radiation NDIR

Sample Supply: Flow injection

The multi N/C pharma offer tailor-made solutions for all pharma-specific TOC applications with full compliance to FDA 21 CFR part 11 and pharmacopoeia regulations.
